General

AI Exchange solves data science problems through a network of experts. Decentralized solutions are immediately production-ready in sponsor's cloud environment.

Challenge sponsors (businesses) post recurrent data science problems and offer a prize pool for each round. They benefit from the best solutions, avoid the burden of model-related technical debt, and reduce to 0 model-related cloud costs.

Experts offer their skills to find solutions and upload their code. If their solution was in the optimal set of solutions for the round, they receive part of the prize pool.
Experts pay for all cloud resources used to run their code.

Intellectual property on both sides (sponsors and experts) is protected by design.

The trait in common with Kaggle Competitions is that experts compete and cooperate to find a solution to a specific problem. That's it.

For Experts
AI Exchange hosts recurrent challenges, this means you have the chance to improve your solution. Moreover, if you find a solution that endures the test of time, you win prizes with no or little effort. AI Exchange protects your intellectual property. Solutions are your own, no one can access your code.

For Businesses
AI Exchange is meant to integrate with your cloud infrastructure. Decentralized solutions are production-ready on your infrastructure. Let your AI teams focus on what they are uniquely positioned to do: data curation, problem setting, and interpretation. Let experts do problem solving.

We have two currencies on the platform: credits and USD. Credits are the digital currency of the platform. With credits experts pay for cloud resources used to run their code. New users automatically get 10 credits. Experts may purchase new credits from the profile page (1 credit = $1). Wallet contains all USD won from past rounds, unless withdrawn. Experts can withdraw money from their wallet at any time. Every withdrawal of less than $20 will have a $3 fee. All withdrawals over $20 are free. To ask for a withdrawal email support@ai-exchange.io.

Drop us a line at support@ai-exchange.io, we'll get back to you as soon as possible.

Business

Have better AI applications, at a fraction of price and time it takes for in-house solutions.

AI teams today must cope with many different problems: data curation, problem setting, problem solving, and interpretation. This is too much. Projects are slower, more expensive, and fail more often. Your team is uniquely positioned to cope with data curation, problem setting, and interpretation. Why? Only they know your business, what you are trying to achieve and why. Conversely, your team is not uniquely positioned to solve AI problems. Many experts know how to solve AI problems, and odds are your team won't come up with the most effective solution.

AI Exchange will let your team focus on what they are uniquely positioned to do and save them a lot of time and code lines.

AI Exchange is useful to any business that routinely collects data on complex phenomena and has one or more AI business cases in mind that are subject to uncertainty. This setting usually means you already have at least a small team working on AI, Data Science, Advanced Analytics, or similar.

Business case examples are: customer churn, cash flow forecasting, anomaly detection, anti-fraud systems, recommender systems. The list is by no means exhaustive!
